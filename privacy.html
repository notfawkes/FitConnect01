Privacy Policy

1. Information We Collect

FitConnect collects the following types of information:

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, and profile information
  • Fitness Data: Activity data, heart rate, sleep patterns, and other health metrics from Fitbit
  • Usage Data: Information about how you use our service

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for:

  • Providing and maintaining our service
  • Processing your Fitbit data integration
  • Improving our service
  • Communicating with you about updates and changes

3. Data Storage and Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information. However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

4. Third-Party Services

Our service integrates with Fitbit. When you connect your Fitbit account, we access and process your Fitbit data according to Fitbit's terms of service and privacy policy.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share your information with:

  • Service providers who assist in operating our service
  • Fitbit, as part of the integration process
  • Legal authorities when required by law

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal information
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Opt-out of data collection

7. Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to track activity on our service and hold certain information. You can instruct your browser to refuse all cookies or to indicate when a cookie is being sent.

8. Children's Privacy

Our service is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
